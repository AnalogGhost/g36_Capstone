Enhance Life Plan

In my earlier years I had several service jobs ranging from serving coffee to landscaping and always loved being able to create things with my hands and build meaningful relationships with the people around me. As I grew and matured I was able to work in managerial and supervisory rolls at different companies with my most recent rolls being at Otter Products in their Customer Service Department. Regardless of what type of job I was working at I realized that personal interaction and the ability to present myself well and build a good reputation was extremely important. In my opinion all jobs require a level of customer service and sales, whether I am selling a product, or selling my ability to perform. I have been fortunate enough to work for small companies or companies that have a culture that is focused around hard work, having fun, enjoying what you do, having work-life balance, and being more focused on people and relationships than dollars and the bottom line. I am really excited and am looking forward to continue my development career.
