G36 Capstone - Enhance Life Plan - Matt Pestridge

Enhance Life Plan (Enhance LP) is an app that helps facilitate the lifeplanning process for users. After registration or login a user can input information about their life currently based on life category, a SWOT categor (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats), and a priority ranking. This information is then processed into a list of top priorities that a user can evaluate to determine what goals they need to set in order to have a healthy and fulfilling life.
