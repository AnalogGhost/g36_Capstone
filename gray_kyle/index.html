Picture search

Using SWIFT for my unfamiliar environment I would create an app that let the users take a photo and have relevant information returned about what is in the picture. More specifically if you took a picture of a business logo the app would analyze the picture and send you back the name of the organization, possible locations, and general information about the organization. You would also be able to get information back about landmarks and nature. This app begins to bridge the gap between augmented reality and the apps we use today. Instead of typing something into google to learn about it with Picture search you look around you, take a picture of something you want to know more about, and without typing you are able to learn something new. I would connect to the Google vision API to understand the images and then possibly other API's to get more information about the picture
