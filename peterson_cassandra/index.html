About my Project: Bag Tags

Description

Bag Tags is a site for disc golfers to trade their bag tags. Bag tags are given out at tournaments or are sold for fundraisers. Each one has a number on it, the lower the number the better the tag.
Currently there is no way to track who has which tag, until now. This app will be a way to track what tags you have and had as well as communicate with people who have the same bag tag as you.

Technologies

  • React/Reduct
  • WebSockets
  • Paypal for payment, https://developer.paypal.com/docs/integration/web/
  • Stripe
