About the Project

My idea involves a "Smart Alarm Clock" concept that I also pitched for the Q3 project here at Galvanize. I've been wanting to become intimate with the concept of mobile application development for a while now, resulting in my interest in this project. The background of the idea comes from people's constant need to improve their time management, and the alarm clock would do the following things:

  • Be able to set up a standard alarm.
  • Be able to preset your locations the night before.
  • Adjust that alarm based on your destinations' morning/early afternoon traffic.
  • Ensure you're never late to your important meeting, sales pitch, or first day of work/school again!

So, as mentioned, it will be able to have preset destinations created the night before by users, this could be a boyfriend/girlfriend's house, a school, a place of work, or really any destination that a user needs to reach in the morning. After setting the destination, and setting their alarm, the alarm will wake up the user "X" amount of time before their pre-set alarm based on the traffic to their destination. This will help them make it to the meetings, first day of school, or the sales pitch and make it on time.
