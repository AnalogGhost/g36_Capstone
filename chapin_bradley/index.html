About my Project...

Back in the early 80's, a simple game was released called Robots. It spawned several different versions and clones, including the one I encountered in the late 80's called Daleks, and I've loved the game ever since.

My intention is to create a newer, more modern looking version of the game, reskinned with art assets produced by my favorite artist (who also happens to be my wife). My re-imagining of this classic will be created with the usual suspects of HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript, alongside the unusual suspect of the HTML framework Phaser.
