Dash&Dine

Dash&Dine is a food tasking app that wil empower small businesses/restaurants to implement their own food delievery app. With a robust feature set, restaurants will have the ability to update their menu, prepare and update orders from an intuative dashboard, where they can also view reports.

Drivers and customers alike will have a mobile app that will help customers filter through their favorite restaurants, and order instantly through their mobile device with payment processing. Drivers respectively will have the ability to view all ready orders from the restaurant, as well as pick them up and use their app to update customers in realtime.

Technologies to be used:

  • Python 3
  • Django
  • Postgres
  • Javascript
  • Swift 3
  • Heorku
