Raised in an Appalachian cave by a very lost tribe of Tibetan Death Buddhists on a steady diet of great comics and terrible movies, this giant mohawked ginger was ensured a comfortable permanent residence on the fringes of society. Having worn out his welcome in the lost city of Atlant(a)is, he entranced a beautiful master chef and two direwolves and led them to the mystical mountains of Colorado, where they now reside. (He also codes a little.)
