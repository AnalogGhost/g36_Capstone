North West Denver Discovery

About the project

Northwest Denver Discovery facilitates the exploration of the the Highlands neighborhood and surrounding areas through data visualization and animation-heavy story-telling. Users are taken on a 'journey' through time and place to different historical landmarks and areas of import and are introduced to the constantly changing demographics of this dynamic area in Colorado's Capital.

Minimum viable product

  • 'Endless Scroll' story-telling and navigable side-bar
  • Interactive Google Map/Custom SVG overlay of landmarks/neighborhoods
  • 'More info' modals for each landmark clicked
  • Dynamically updating, mini, sticky side-view map of entire area in context of where you are in exploration
  • Database with relevant information for each landmark/area, including description, name, geo-location etc...
  • Graphical representation of demographic data (home price, revenue, number of stores built etc...)
  • Contact section with information about developer
  • Historical and present-day images to help tell story

Additional features

  • Story-telling section with 'flippable' pages of highlighted individuals throughout
  • "TIME MAP" of specific areas that have seen signigicant growth with scrollable 'timeline' showing buildings built, destroyed, and restored.

About the developer

A life-long resident of the Highlands neighborhood, I have a vested interest in Northwest Denver. The goal of this project is educational in nature, and is intended as a learning tool for current and future neighbors, real-estate agents, historical enthusiasts and Denver-ites to get involved with history in a fun and engaging way.
Further, this site will be available to any of the neighborhood associations and historical societies of Denver, should they wish to feature it for their own purposes.
