Woodworking Planner

(Official name pending)

Summary

Planning a woodworking project requires a lot of math, but with a little bit of geometry knowledge it's easy to calulate the dimensions of your components and the angles at which everything fits together. The tricky part is determining the right amount of lumber to buy. Even the most experienced woodworkers often just mark up their calculations by 20%-30% to avoid going back to the hardware store.

This application will utilize an online 3D editor to allow woodworkers to plan their projects in the most realistic way possible. Once designs are complete, the application will take the dimensions of all the components and find the most efficient use of board space to minimize waste and cost. The app will also integrate with Home Depot's API to find corresponding materials and estimate cost.
