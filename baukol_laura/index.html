Northland Chorale

About the Northland Chorale

Since 1970, the Northland Chorale community show choir has been entertaining audiences with music and dance in a fun-filled, family friendly show. A 501(c)3 nonprofit organization, the Northland Chorale is in need of a new website to showcase the organization and to attract audiences to its biannual shows.

Project Information

The Northland Chorale board of directors has asked me to create a new website for the choir as my capstone project. The project site will consist of a Home page, an About Us page, and a Show Information and Tickets page that includes functionality for audience members to purchase tickets to the April 2017 show. After completion of the capstone presentation, the site will be updated to include additional informational pages as well as a login section for choir members to have access to member only information and files.

MVP Features

  • Home page with information about the Chorale and the upcoming show, and a photo carousel.
  • About Us page with more information about the choir, the music director, and accompanist with photos.
  • Show Information and Tickets page with information about the show, the show flyer, and a way (using Stripe or PayPal) for audience members to purchase tickets for the upcoming show.
  • Link to the Northland Chorale Facebook page on each page of the site.
  • Contact information with email address link for the Chorale on each page of the site.
  • Design using layout, colors, graphics, and images that provide a good UI/UX.

Stretch Goals

  • Map to theatre location on the Show Information and Tickets page.
  • Video footage of the choir on the About Us page.
  • Member only section using authentication and authorization.
