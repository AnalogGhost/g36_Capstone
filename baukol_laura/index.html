Northland Chorale

About the Northland Chorale

Since 1970, the Northland Chorale show choir has been entertaining audiences with live music and dance via fun-filled, family friendly shows. A 501(c)3 nonprofit organization, the Northland Chorale is in need of a new website to showcase the organization and to attract audiences to its biannual performances.

Project Information

The Northland Chorale board of directors has asked me to create a new website for the choir as my capstone project. The project site will consist of a Home page, a Who We Are page, and a Tickets page with information about an upcoming show and that includes functionality for audience members to purchase tickets. After completion of the capstone presentation, the site will be updated to include additional informational pages as well as a login section for choir members to have access to member only information and files.

MVP for Capstone

  • Home (splash) page with information about the Chorale and the upcoming show
  • Who We Are page with more information about the choir, the music director, accompanist, and board of directors with a photo carousel
  • Tickets page with information about the show, and a way (using Stripe or PayPal) for audience members to purchase tickets for an upcoming show
  • Link to the Northland Chorale facebook page on each page of the site
  • Contact information with email address link for the Chorale on each page of the site
  • Responsive design using layout, colors, graphics, and images that provide a good UI/UX

Stretch Goals

  • Map to theatre location on the Tickets page
  • Mailing list sign-up functionality
  • Photo with caption that pops up in a modal when a photo is clicked
  • Video compilation of the choir
  • Member only section using authentication and authorization
