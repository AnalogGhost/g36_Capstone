Lazy Buffs Data Analytics


Abstract :

Lazy Buffs is a Galvanize-based food and alcohol delivery service that connects Boulder restaurants with reliable drivers ready to deliver to lazy customers.

I implemented data analytics pages across three separate Lazy Buffs apps in order to give their dispatch team, restaurants, and drivers a way to visualize their monthly revenue with Lazybuffs.

The application considers data on orders totals and subtotals, deliver dates and times, and tips and provide both restauranters and delivery drivers with data-driven visualizations of their profitability.

I also worked with a senior developer to simplify store billing procedures by using the SendGrid API to automatically send a dynamically genrated monthly invoice.



Technologies:

  • React with JSX and ES6
  • Recharts - D3 Component Wrapper for React
  • Sendgrid API - Email and Notification Automation
  • Webpack - Module Bundler
